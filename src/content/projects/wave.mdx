# Wave Unified Platform

Wave is FireBytes' flagship unified platform that revolutionizes smart building management by consolidating multiple building systems into a single, intelligent ecosystem. This comprehensive platform enables enterprises to streamline operations, enhance security, and optimize efficiency through seamless integration and advanced automation.

## Platform Overview

Wave serves as the central nervous system for smart buildings, integrating access control, environmental monitoring, energy management, and space analytics into one cohesive platform. Designed for modern enterprises, Wave transforms traditional building management into an intelligent, data-driven operation.
