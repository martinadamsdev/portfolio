# Unionfab Digital Manufacturing Platform

A comprehensive digital manufacturing platform developed for Unionfab, China's leading 3D printing manufacturing company. The platform revolutionizes traditional manufacturing by providing instant quotes, automated production workflows, and seamless order management for global enterprises.

## Project Overview

Unionfab operates 800+ industrial-grade 3D printing equipment and serves over 80,000 enterprises worldwide. This platform digitizes their entire manufacturing process, from initial quote generation to final product delivery, creating an end-to-end manufacturing ecosystem.

### Advanced Quote Engine
- **Instant Pricing** - Real-time cost calculation based on geometry analysis
- **Material Optimization** - Intelligent material selection recommendations
- **Process Selection** - Automated manufacturing process optimization
